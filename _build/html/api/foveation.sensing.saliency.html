Sample from log probabilities (robust to many bins and small probabilities).

+-inf and nan will be interpreted as zero probability

Parameters:

  • logits (torch.Tensor) – Tensor of shape (batch_size, num_points)

  • coords (torch.Tensor) – Tensor of shape (batch_size, num_points, 2) containing x,y coordinates

  • size (int) – Number of samples per batch item (default: 1)

  • tmp_scale (float, optional) – Scale parameter for exponential distribution

  • bounding_coords (tuple, optional) – Bounding coordinates for fixations

  • deterministic (bool) – Whether to sample deterministically as the argmax over logits (default: False)

Returns:

Tuple of (sampled_indices, updated_logits) where:

sampled_indices (torch.Tensor): Tensor of shape (batch_size, size) containing sampled point indices updated_logits (torch.Tensor): Tensor of shape (batch_size, num_points) with IOR applied
